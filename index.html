Our Facilities

Comfortable Living Spaces

Private and semi-private rooms with emergency call systems, comfortable furniture, and personal temperature control. Each room is designed for safety and comfort.

Dining & Social Areas

Beautiful dining rooms serving nutritious meals prepared by our chef, plus common areas for socializing, activities, and family visits. Our dining experience promotes community and enjoyment.

Outdoor Spaces

Secure gardens, walking paths with handrails, and comfortable patio areas with shade for enjoying fresh air and nature. Our outdoor spaces are designed for safety and relaxation.
